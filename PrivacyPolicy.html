Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026. This policy explains how LuisDev apps handle data, advertising, analytics, purchases, and support requests.

LuisDev games are generally offered as free, ad-supported mobile games. Some games may include optional in-app purchases or platform-specific features.

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to mobile games and related support pages published by LuisDev or Luis_Dev, including games listed on Google Play and other official store pages.

Information We Collect

LuisDev does not ask users to create a direct account on this website. Depending on the app, platform, device settings, and third-party services used, the following categories of information may be processed:

  • Device information such as device model, operating system, language, app version, and approximate technical identifiers.
  • Advertising information such as advertising ID, ad interactions, and data used to limit, deliver, measure, or improve ads.
  • Usage information such as app activity, session events, feature interactions, gameplay progress, and performance events.
  • Diagnostics such as crash logs, performance data, error reports, and stability information.
  • Approximate location inferred by platform services or ad partners, such as country or region, when permitted by your device settings and applicable law.
  • Purchase information handled by Google Play, Apple App Store, or another platform when optional purchases are available.
  • Contact information you choose to send by email or through a store support channel.

How We Use Information

Information is used to operate, maintain, protect, and improve the apps. This includes:

  • Showing and measuring ads.
  • Providing gameplay features and app functionality.
  • Finding and fixing bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
  • Responding to support, privacy, or business messages.
  • Complying with platform requirements, fraud prevention, security, and legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

LuisDev apps may use third-party services that process information under their own privacy policies. These services may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Billing, Apple App Store, or equivalent storefront services when purchases are available.

Third-party services may collect information directly from your device to provide ads, analytics, crash reporting, purchases, fraud prevention, and platform services. Their practices can vary by region, device settings, consent choices, and app version.

Advertising

Some LuisDev apps are ad-supported. Advertising partners may use device identifiers, approximate location, app activity, and other signals to deliver, limit, personalize, or measure ads. You can manage ad personalization and advertising identifiers through your device settings and platform account controls.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Mobile apps do not use browser cookies in the same way websites do. However, third-party SDKs may use local storage, device identifiers, or similar technologies to provide ads, analytics, security, and app functionality.

Data Sharing

LuisDev does not sell personal information. Data may be shared with service providers, ad networks, analytics providers, storefronts, hosting providers, or legal authorities when needed to operate the apps, comply with law, protect rights, prevent fraud, or respond to user requests.

Data Retention

Data is kept only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law, platform policy, dispute resolution, security, or legitimate business needs. Third-party services may maintain their own retention schedules.

Children's Privacy

LuisDev apps are not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent where required. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, objection, or restriction of certain personal information. You can also manage many choices directly through your device, Google Play, Apple App Store, and ad personalization settings.

To make a privacy request, contact us at luisdevcontacto@gmail.com. We may need enough information to verify and process your request.

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information. No internet transmission, app, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

International Transfers

Apps and third-party services may process information in countries different from where you live. By using the apps, information may be transferred and processed where LuisDev or service providers operate, subject to applicable legal safeguards.

External Links

Apps, ads, store pages, or this website may link to third-party websites or services. LuisDev is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of those third parties.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the apps after changes means the updated policy applies.

Contact
